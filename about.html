How I Got Started

I'm a Software Engineer from NYC trying to make an impact on the world through the things I create.

I was always interested in how technology could be used to make life easier, and as I delved deeper into the world of software engineering, I became fascinated with the idea of automating mundane tasks and creating solutions for local businesses. It first started during the pandemic when I walked into one of my favorite restaurants and noticed they had a long line of customers with no end in sight. They told me it was due to the fact they didn't have enough staff to accomodate all the customer traffic.

I knew there had to be a better way so over the next few weeks, I worked tirelessly on a project that would alleviate tedious tasks being done manually (writing down the orders, answering phone calls, delivering the written orders to the kitchen, etc). As I implemented more features I noticed I was making a real difference in the business's day-to-day operations and I was hooked on the idea of using technology to create solutions that could help people thrive. From that day on, I knew I wanted to create technology that would reduce the amount of time spent on tedious tasks and redirect that time towards meaningful interactions that only a real person could offer.
